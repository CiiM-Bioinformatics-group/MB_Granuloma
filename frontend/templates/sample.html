We provide access to detailed metadata and quality-controlled spatial transcriptomics data from 39 FFPE tissue samples collected across three institutions (Aachen, MHH and Yale). Users can explore information about each sample, including tissue type, cohort classification (discovery or validation), patient demographics, and data quality metrics such as gene detection rates, and spatial spot coverage. This module enables in-depth exploration of the dataset and supports hypothesis generation and validation across diverse patient samples.

🧬 Samples are grouped by tissue type and mycobacterial species into four categories. Click the ICON to explore the data of lymph node, pulmonary, MTB, or NTM granulomas.
