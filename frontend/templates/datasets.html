Spatial Transcriptomics Dataset Browser

stRNAAtlas provides a manually curated compilation of spatial transcriptomics (ST) data spanning a wide variety of biological systems. We collected 40 ST datasets comprising a lot sections across organs, developmental stages and pathological states from humans. All curated ST datasets were processed by a centralized workflow. We provide this interface to help users navigate through this catalog. Select a ST dataset and explore the spatially resolved expression profiles of genes of interest.
